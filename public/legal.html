Compliance

NorthStar is designed to help contractors comply with applicable telemarketing and privacy regulations. However, it is your responsibility to ensure compliance with:

  • Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)
  • CAN-SPAM Act
  • State-specific call recording consent laws (one-party vs. two-party consent)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) if serving EU customers
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) if serving California residents

Consult with legal counsel to ensure your use of our platform complies with all applicable laws.

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

We strive for 99.9% uptime for our call handling service. In the event of verified downtime exceeding 0.1% in a calendar month, you may request a service credit proportional to the downtime. Planned maintenance (notified 48 hours in advance) is excluded.

Acceptable Use

Our platform must be used ethically and lawfully. Prohibited uses include:

  • Using the AI for deceptive, fraudulent, or abusive purposes
  • Collecting sensitive data (SSNs, medical records, payment card numbers) through the AI without proper safeguards
  • Knowingly misrepresenting the AI as a human to callers in jurisdictions where disclosure is required
  • Using the platform to harass, intimidate, or spam

Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of our policies or applicable law through the use of our platform, please report it immediately to support@northstar-ai.com.
