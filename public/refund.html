Refund Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

NorthStar Solutions is committed to providing exceptional value. This Refund Policy outlines the terms for cancellations and refunds.

14-Day Free Trial

All new accounts start with a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required during the trial period. You may cancel at any time during the trial with no charge.

Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions are billed in advance. If you cancel your subscription:

  • You will retain access to the service until the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds are provided for the remaining days in the billing period
  • You will not be charged for the following month

Annual Subscriptions

If you have an annual plan and cancel within the first 30 days, you will receive a full refund. After 30 days, refunds are prorated based on the remaining months in your billing period, minus a 10% administrative fee.

How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Alternatively, you can contact us at support@northstar-ai.com and we will process the cancellation for you.

Service Issues

If you experience a significant service outage or technical issue that prevents you from using the platform, please contact our support team. We will investigate and, at our discretion, may issue a partial or full refund for the affected period.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days and returned to the original payment method. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been issued.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We will notify customers of material changes via email.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at support@northstar-ai.com or visit our Contact page.
