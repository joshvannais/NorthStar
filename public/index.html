Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does the AI Office Manager work?

When a customer calls your business number, the AI answers using a natural, professional voice. It asks about their needs, collects their contact info, and takes notes — just like a real receptionist. You get the details instantly via text and email.

What do I need to set up on my end?

Just enter your business phone number and you're all set. Your AI Office Manager starts answering calls immediately — no equipment, no software, no forwarding, no new numbers to hand out. Existing customers and known contacts are automatically recognized and ring through to you as usual.

What if the call is an emergency or urgent?

The AI is trained to recognize urgency and flag it. You'll get an immediate notification with the details so you can call back right away if needed.
